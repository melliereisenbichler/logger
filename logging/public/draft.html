Welcome to Cookin' on Rails

Who doesn't know this problem: the fridge is full, but you have no idea what to cook from all these ingredients?

This is where Cookin' on Rails can help you:

You can check all the ingredients you have at home and Cookin' on Rails searches the database for recipes you can cook from them. Or you can share you own recipes on this platform.
